Dungeon Crawler Project

The following project is a first person 2d dungeon crawler.


Click here to access the game.

CONTROLS

Much like other role playing games, this game works around menus.
Use the arrow keys to move the cursor around the menus and press Z to confirm.
In town you can purchase items with Gold (upper right) and talk to people.
When you're ready to enter the dungeon, select Depart from the town menu.
In dungeon, use the left and right arrow keys to turn, forward and backward to move.
Press Z to open your menu and use purchased items.
Sometimes when you walk you'll encounter monsters, in which menus open with options.
Select Attack to deal damage, and Magic to deal greater damage, however this costs MP
which is represented by the blue bar. If you don't have enough you wont be able to use it.
Once all enemies are defeated, you earn Gold which you can use to purchase more items.
If your characters' HP bar reaches 0 (green bar), they die. If both characters die you get
a game over.

Good luck exploring!
